About

Hi, I'm Riin Valena - a BSIT Specializing in Web and Mobile Application Development student from Far Eastern University, currently based in Tondo, Manila. I specialize in web design and development, with hands-on experience using languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also have a strong creative side, with skills in Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and other multimedia tools that help bring ideas to life visually and interactively.

More about my process

When I begin a website I first imagine what it must do which serves as the core functions users need and the journeys they’ll take. With that map in mind I sketch a wireframe that becomes my blueprint: simple, structured, and focused on purpose. Next I plan how to build it, which technologies, how to break tasks into steps, and where to optimize for performance and accessibility. Alongside that technical plan I look for moments to bring in multimedia; images, motion, and sound, and weave my Photoshop and video skills into the experience so visuals support interaction. Finally I treat each project as a chance to push my boundaries: trying a new pattern, streamlining a flow, or refining an animation until it feels right.
